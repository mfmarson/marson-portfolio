About Me

With over 10 years in healthcare, I have had the privilege of working directly with patients and serving in operational capacities. After five years filling in-person roles, my journey led me to the dynamic world of virtual behavioral health start-ups. There, I supported clinical and business operations, served as a virtual clinic manager, assisted with PR and marketing, mapped patient journeys, and hand tested and selected all required medical applications.

A brief exploration into healthcare tech revealed my passion for development. I took a software development course, graduated from a 12-week immersive Fullstack bootcamp, and it has been history since. Leveraging my expertise in Python, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, SQL, and React, combined with my healthcare background, I bring a diverse skill set to the table. I am excited to apply these skills to create efficient, user-friendly software solutions.
